30 April 2003

From The Brambles

Maybe you remember me and my inquisitive e-mails!

I have been meaning to e-mail you for a while, but I had leads to other branches of my family history and have been busy with these.
I thought it only fair after you have shared so much with me to update you on Flora Titheridge (daughter of William Titheridge and Sarah Ann Keeling).

Born: 3 April 1864
Died: 23 February 1954
Married: James Hedges, 27 December 1886
She had 9 children

Florence Emma - October 12 1887
Henry James (Harry) - July 6 1889
Stillborn child - January 26 1892
Ethel Kate - August 19 1893
Lilian Jane - October 17 1894
George Leonard - January 28
Regie Victor - March 5 1899
Winifred - August 12 1901
William Herbert - April 17 1903 - my grandfather.

All of the above dates are from certificates/family bible On the 1881 census, Flora is a servant to Frederick T Clark, although her age is shown as 19 yrs. After speaking to my dad, he can remember her sister Kate as quite strict and austere, but didn't realize she had a daughter.

I hope the above fills the gap regarding Flora. I may well be in contact again, depending on where my info takes me.

Theresa
