Hi,-
In my family history research I find that I link into the Titheridge family through Anne Titheridge born in Ropley, Hants in 1804. She is I believe the daughter of the Richard Titheridge Jan 24 1779 of Cheriton Hants whose wife's name was Mary. Anne Titheridge married a William Oakley of Ropley and their daughter Harriett is my great, great grandmother.
