Oh dear, My aunt. Never met her..several stories about Minnie. Can't confirm any. In 1913 she married Ernest P. Seward. My impression was she moved to the United States for a while. She may worked at Kip's Castle in Montclair, NJ, USA.

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kip%27s_Castle_Park

She had a son, born  in 1912, Ernest Percival Tedridge. A story has Ernest visiting the family home on Silverdale Rd. Southampton. Apparently an Amercan serviceman he was not received by the family.
