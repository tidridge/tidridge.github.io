WILLIAM JOHN TITHERADGE

REGIMENT: Royal Field Artillery

Records Found: Medal Card and Medal Rolls and Army Service Record

Medals Awarded: Victory Medal and British medal

Military Information: William enlisted on 29 February 1916 aged 18. His previous trade was a shunter on the Midland Railway. On enlistment his address was 19 Caledonian St. Kings Cross, London, NW.

His service records shows:

At home 29-2-1916 until 5-9-1917, France 6-9-17 until 14-11-17, Italy 15-11-1917 until 27-3-1918, France 28-3-1918-18010-1918, Home 19-10-1918 to 25-10-1918 France 26-10-1918 to 1-4-1918 Germany 2-4-1919 to 16-10-1919 Home 17-10-1919

Family Information: Willaim was born on i February 1898 in Pancras LOndon, son of William Daniel Titheradge  and Annie Johanna Bloodworth. He married Florence Bella James on 25 October 1918 in All Saints, Islington. THey had three girls and two boys. William died 2 May 1977 in Dagenham, Essex.
