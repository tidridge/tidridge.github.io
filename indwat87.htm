Medals awarded:Victory medal:yes

Other military information:

Joined up 13 November 1915 aged 21. Address on enlistment 5 Shanklin Road, Sunday’s Well, Cork. Former occupation a clerk / grocer’s assistant. Single. Described as 5 foot 11 ½ inches with 36 inch chest. Next of Kin is his father William Tidridge at the above address. James' address in May 1919 was Mill House, Doneraile, County Cork.

He was on dangerously ill list at the Australian Hospital in Wimereux with influenza. Removed from list 2 December 1918. He was discharged on 14 February 1919 aged 24. "Sick and wounded" is stamped on his record
