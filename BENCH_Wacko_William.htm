William WACKO Bench 
[52 Street and Ada Boulevard]
 

 

 

 

 

 

The Wacko sit on a downward slope from Ada Boulevard, the bench faces Southwest offering a good view of the 'land across the river' but, unfortu-nately, over time this will be lost as the trees on the north bank of the North Saskatchewan River continue to grow. Taking photographs of views from the bench in early April provides but a glimpse of what the view is like in summer. 

So far more information is not forth coming...hopefully, if contact can be made with the family more insights will come available.
