As promised I advise a brief description of myself and immediate family, and my Titheridge pedigree.
FULL NAME - Henry Ross Titheridge (commonly known as Ross)
BORN 04/10/1931
OCCUPATION - Retired bank clerk (Bank of New Zealand)
MARITAL STATUS -Married 03/06/1966 to Doris Merle Canton (born 07/11/1935)
FAMILY - 2 sons
No 1. Mark Albert, single (born 09/12/1967)
No 2. Grant Henry (born 09/09/1969), married 12/09/1998 to Belinda Joan Smith (born 29/12/1975). No children.
RESIDENTIAL ADDRESS - 19 Locksley Avenue, CHRISTCHURCH 8006, New Zealand.

My wife and I live in a brick bungalow overlooking the Avon River in the suburb of Dallington which is approximately 8 kilometers from the center of the city of Christchurch.

TITHERIDGE CONNECTIONS.

I am a great grandson of James Titheridge and Eliza Rossi(Rossey) the widow of Thomas Harfield to whom she had 3 children. James and Eliza had 6 children, William, James, Frank and Henry all born in England, the other two Edward (or Edwin) and Lousia were born later after the family had arrived in New Zealand.

Henry (born 24/09/1872) was married to Nellie Matilda Johnson (born 26/11/1879) on 09/06/1902 and had 2 children, a son and a daughter. The son Albert George, my father, was born on 21/04/1903 and married on 26/09/1926 to Iris Elaine Pike (born 28/01/1905) and they had 2 sons, my brother Albert Noel (born 25/06/1927) and me.
Seeing that my father and the father of Neil Titheridge were cousins I guess that makes Neil and I second cousins.

Save for three months compulsory military training as an 18 year old I have had no other military experience.
Religious affiliation; Christened C of E.

I note we differ in naming the husband of Eliza Rossi - William or James, on their move to New Zealand.

Regards, Ross
