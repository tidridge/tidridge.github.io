 17 March 2004

From: Melifj@aol.com
To: jtidridge

Hi there

I have just discovered your web site,and have spent ages looking at all the wonderful info. I thought somebody out there may be interested and have some knowledge of the names that I am about to mention. I was born Melanie Faith Titheridge in Camberwell Green, London, on the 4/12/57.

My father's name was Peter Lionel Titheridge and he was married to Rose----- and had two daughters, called Valerie and Gillian before he married my mother, Sheila Graham. My father has a brother called Anthony and he has four sons, Peter,Geoffrey,Alan and .Andrew.

My father and uncle's father was called William Henry Titheridge, and all I know was that he was born 1800 and something. William H's father and mother were a George and Georgina Titheridge and as far as I know, they had eight children. I only know some of their names, William Henry (my grand-father) Ernie, Lilian, Sam and Joseph. They lived in London,whereabouts, I do not know. I do have a wonderful photograph of my great-grandparents with their children.

My grandfather William received the Distinguished Conduct Medal during the first world war. The citation in the supplement to the London Gazette dated 1.5.1918 reads as follows;

"His Majesty the King has been pleased to award the Distinguished Conduct medal to the undermentioned for gallantry and distinguished service in the field for conspicuous gallantry and devotion to duty. During a mounted attack a horse carrying a machine gun broke loose. After several attempts he succeeded in catching it among the trenches occupied by the enemy and brought it in under heavy fire. He showed splendid coolness and resource".

Melanie Titheridge

note : We hold most of the basic details of this branch of the family and will shortly add what we know as an extra page to the family tree.(jt)
