Dayrell Charles John Titheridge
Served in the Royal Artillery 2nd Field Regiment.
Rank of Lance Bombardier, Service number 794755.

 

Died during WW II on 12 September 1939 aged 24. He is buried at Shedfield at the churchyard of St John the Baptist Church. Dayrell was born 28 January 1915 at Droxford, the son of Noah John and Annie Louisa Titheridge of Waltham Chase and the husband of Jessie M Webb. They were married in 1935.
