W HERE IT ALL BEGAN I think it important to include a little background on the places my/your ancestors came from: John [1643-1708] and Ann Titheridge nee Quallatt [?-1702] our first known ancestors, were from Cheriton a village situated close to Winchester, Hampshire, United Kingdom. Go here to read about the battle of Cheriton: https://weaponsandwarfare.com/2019/09/13/the-battle-of-cheriton-i/

Cheriton was a small village, perhaps just big enough to support John in his tailoring business. John and Ann had seven children, and their first child died under the age of one year.

There are nine generations going back from my Dad, Walter Sidney to John of 1643. In that nine generations there are two Johns, three Williams, one Henry, two Harrys one Walter. These Dads shared a variety of surnames: Titheridge, Tytheridge, Tethreg, Tythereg. These are by no means the last of the names ascribed to the family. These discrepancies are blamed on the fact many people could not read or write: When asked their surnames, the official [who could read and write] would have to guess the spelling. In some cases there are different spellings within the same family. 1
