March 04, 2010

From John Tidridge": Nothing further has been heard from Raymond, so based on Mike and Anne's recommendation[?] the family tree has been completed. 

Year 2000: From Mike and Ann Titheradge

We recently had an interesting Email from a Raymond Tidridge of Swansea. He is interested in family history and has traced his family line back to Alfred Tidbridge marrying Jane Wakely on 24th December 1888 at Swansea. Alfred's age on the marriage certificate suggests he was born in 1855. His father's name is given as Henry. It looks as though Alfred could be the son of Henry Tidridge and Ann Newell, although there is a discrepancy of 5 years in the birth date! We have suggested to Raymond that he tries looking up the 1891 census to try and find out some more details about Alfred.

We have passed on your name and email address to Raymond, so hopefully he will be contacting you. We have also directed him to your website.
