FROM THE DIRECTORS.

GET FIT TUESDAYS

Get Fit Tuesdays resumes on September 13 at 6:30 p.m.at Highlands.

The hour-long exercise class is designed for all levels of fitness and is led by Gail Rogers, a certified personal trainer.

Bring a water bottle, good shoes, comfortable clothing, and a willingness to work and have fun. The cost is free, but you can make a donation if you would like.

LIFE TOGETHER

Through the voices of civic leaders, theologians and local practitioners, this event aims to paint the theological and 'how-to' picture for local churches on how to be active participants in the building and transformation of their communities.

Life Together will be held on Thursday October 13 at the Edmonton City Hall. Registration deadline is September 26.

For additional information and to register, visit theurbanforum.com/local-info/life-together
