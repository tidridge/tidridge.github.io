Hi,

I have begun to research my family tree and have found out that you have already done it! What a nice treat to read about the UK side of the family. I am a descendant from the 3rd generation number 25 item. My grandparents were Elsie Beatrice Titheradge who married Gilbert Emanuel Parkes.

Please do no think me too presumptuous, but I noticed that you have my father's name down as Jack Parkes where he was actually registered John Gilbert Parkes born in 1921, married Edna Joyce Clarke. If you need any further information that I may have please do not hesitate to contact me. I also have updated dates for children, grandchildren etc if needed.

It was interesting to read the way the spelling of the surname changed over the years, here we use Titheradge and the family is well represented out west in NSW Australia.

Thanking you again for all your hard work.

HELEN SMITH (nee Parkes)
Email: smithhj@bigpond.net.au
