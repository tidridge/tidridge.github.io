PARK- Ada Boulevard 51 Street 

 
 
 
 
 

 In 2020 the City of Edmonton decided to enhance the vacant land on Ada Boulevard near 51 Street and to create develop a park. The park is near the McAra Mememorial  Bench.
 
 
 
 
 

 

September 2020: First photo shows the park the east end of the park, the second shows the west end, both looking south. As can been seen by the trees the park has been under construction for some time/
