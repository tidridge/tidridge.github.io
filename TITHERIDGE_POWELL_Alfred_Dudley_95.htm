Regiment/regiments) or Corps: Australian Engineers (1st Anzac Wireless Section / 4th Field Artillery Brigade) Regimental No: 6426 Rank/Ranks: Corporal

Medals awarded: Distinguished Conduct Medal (DCM) Any other military information: Extract taken from the London Gazette under heading Australian Imperial force; DCM award 6426 Cpl. A. D. Titheridge, Engrs. “For conspicuous gallantry and devotion to duty in repeatedly repairing wireless aerial when broken by heavy shell fire until it became impossible to keep it working. He then established successful communication with amplifier, and continued to work for two days .after his ration supply had been destroyed by shell fire, eventually bringing away the whole of his wireless gear through heavy shelling.”
