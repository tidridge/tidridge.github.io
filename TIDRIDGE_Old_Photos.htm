SOME OLD, OLD PICTURES, SOME OVER ONE HUNDRED YEARS OLD... TIDRIDGES AND RELATIVES, FROM FLORENCE HAVEN, NEW JERSEY, USA

 

JANUARY 2013 John Tidridge received over 30 old family pictures from Florence Haven, New Jersey, USA. Some of the people have been identified and placed in the proper spots on their family pages.

However, several photos have people who have not been identified. They, together with those recognized, have been placed on this page in the hope someone might just recognize the people/place. Enjoy
