Arthur Robert TITHERADGE
(c 1887- June 9, 1917) Gunner, 234th Seige Battery,

Royal Garrision Battery 

 
 
 
 

LEST WE FORGET

The Following Notes relate to Arthur Robert Titheradge (c 1887-June 9, 1917), a gunner with the 234th Siege Battery, Royal Garrison battery. Arthur died in France in W.W. I.) The wife of Arthur was Mabel Catherine, (nee Bramley) (1910-?) they lived in West Dulwich, London, UK. His parents were Robert Arthur and Alice A.M (nee Viney). He had a brother William C (1888-?) and a sister, Dorothy (1896-?)
