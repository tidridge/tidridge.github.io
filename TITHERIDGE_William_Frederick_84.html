William Frederick TITHERIDGE possibly1884-1941

 

 

September 2021: From  Ann Titheradge

Is anyone in the group related to a W F Titheridge who was a Corporal in the Royal Engineers in World War 1? If so you may like to know that his Silver War medal is currently on sale on ebay for £25.
 
I don't know who W F Titheridge is, but my best guess is that he is William Frederick Titheridge born in 1884 in Winchester son of Thomas and Sophia Titheridge and died in Winchester in 1941. If anyone can confirm this suggestion (or even prove me wrong) I would love to hear from you.
