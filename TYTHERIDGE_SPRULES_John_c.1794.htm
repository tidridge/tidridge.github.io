Taken from Ann Titheradges site typed April 29, 2020

As I am sitting at home I decided to peruse some information I have on my un-attached Tytheridge family. I wonder if anyone can help me?

My John Tytheridge married Ann Sprules in 1816 at Christchurch with St. Mary and St. Stephens, Spitalfields - Sprules is a Scottish name and some of the witnesses are MacGregors.

Ann was born in Mitchum, Surrey in 1786 - her father is William Sprules. I can't find a birth certificate for John.

Ann and John had at least two children:

John born 1820(?)

Ann [my gg grandmother] 1821.

In 1835 John was apprenticed to a pattern maker in the City of London. His father is said to be a resident of Windsor, Berkshire.

In the 18411 census John and Ann areliving together at 139 The Minories St. Bodolph, Aldgate. John is a Taylor. Their is no mention of their parents.

Ann married William Bennett in 1841 wirth John being a witness. Ann emigrated to Australia with her hiusband in 1852.

1844-48 Tax Recordsa have John living in the Minories, Portsoken ward. He is now trading as a tailor.

My query is:

1. Does anyone know of or is connected to a John Tytheridge who was a tailer in London at this time?

2. Anyone know of the Tytheridges connected to the Sprules family.

Thank you,

Rose.
