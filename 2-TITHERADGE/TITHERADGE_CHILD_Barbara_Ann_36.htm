Barbara Ann TITHERADGE,(1936-1941)
civilian

 
 
 
 
 

LEST WE FORGET

Barbara died in 1941 as the result of enemy action over London.  She was the daughter of John Alfred and Florence Titheradge. They lived in Woodford Essex, UK.
