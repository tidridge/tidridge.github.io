William WACKO Bench 
[52 Street and Ada Boulevard]
 

 

 

 

 

 

The Wacko bench faces Southwest offering a good view of the 'land across the river' but, unfortunately, over time this will be lost as the trees on the north bank of the North Saskatchewan River continue to grow. Taking photographs of views from the bench in early April provides but a glimpse of what the view is like in summer.
