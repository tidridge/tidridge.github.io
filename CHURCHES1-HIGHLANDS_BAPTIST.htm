INTRODUCING REV. DUANE I. BERRY, Pastor Highlands Baptist Church.
 

 

             Rev. Duane Berry was born and raised in Moncton, New Brunswick. He attended the United Baptist Bible School during which time he was licensed to preach by his home Church, Highfield Baptist Church. He attend-ed Cordon College majoring in Christian Education. He was active in athletics, music and student government, being President of the student body in his senior year. Following graduation in 1963, he attended Gordon - Conwell Seminary and served as student Pastor of a small Covenant Church. Graduating in 1967, he became Minister of Education at Ruggles Baptist Church, Boston, Mass. Then for three yea::s was Minister of Education & Youth at Grace Chapel in Lexington, Mass. In 1971, he became pastor of Trinity Baptist Church, Nashua, N. H.

              Sandra (Rile) and Duane met in college and were married in 1964. Sandra received her B.S. in Elemen-tary Education from Gordon College. She is a native of flue Bell, Pa.(Philadelphia area). She has had an active ministry through her ventriloquism and music, both singing and playing the piano and teaching.

               The Berrys have four children, Barclay 11, Bradley 10, Stephanie 8 and Brett 4.
