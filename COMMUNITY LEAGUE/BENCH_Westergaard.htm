Ernie Westergaard  Ernie Westergaard grew up on a farm in Denmark. He came to Canada in 1957, in search of a new life and a brighter future.

He worked at a flower shop in Edmonton when he first arrived, then went to Uranium City, Saskatchewan to work as a miner.

He  then returned to Edmonton as a bus driver for ETS, and then finally as an ow

ner operator trucking in Western Canada's oil and gas industry. He met his wife, Lissi – also form Denmark – in Uranium City.

He is survived by his wife, two children, Sandy and Dennis, and a granddaughter, Erin.
