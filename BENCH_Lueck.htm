Claus LUECK Bench 
[72 Street and Ada Boulevard]
 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

The Lueck Bench is situated on the south side of Ada Boulevard in the Concordia University area, around 71 Street. It is and will continue to be, a busy area with lots of students and residents taking advantage of the slendid walhking area. Thew photograph shows [barely!] some of the homes south across the North Saskatchewan River.
