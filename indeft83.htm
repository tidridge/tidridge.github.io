from "Susan L Bramley" 1forall@amserve.net

Hi John and Maureen
Hope all is well in sunny Canada!!
If it s anything like over here it s very far from sunny!

I have been at Notts Archives today trying to find some info on Edith Florence Titheridge and Frederick Bell First of all it looks like I have found their other children but I need to double check by looking at baptism records first but everything seems too coincidental for them not to be. I have been searching all afternoon for Frederick's second marriage without success so I thought if I can find Edith's death this may give me an idea. Again without success, I have looked between 1916 and 1922 to no avail.

Can anyone please help?

It seems Frederick did not want to be found, first no record of marriage, second he was not on the electoral role from 1915 to 1935. What I am wondering is, did Edith die as young as we thought? Did Frederick not remarry but live in sin because Edith was still alive? I have no idea!

I am so desperately trying to find a death date for Edith Florence Bell nee Titheridge and if anyone can help I would be eternally grateful.

Many thanks forever.

su
